About

There are thousands of Grateful Dead soundboard recordings on the Internet Archive. That's incredible — and a little overwhelming.

XLIIs organizes them into collections. Four at a time, each focused on a different era, with write-ups that give you historical context for every show. The collections rotate every three months, so there's always a reason to dig into a period you might have overlooked.

No account. No tracking. Just pick a collection and press play.
