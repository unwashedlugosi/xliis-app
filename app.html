The App

XLIIs is an iPhone app.

Four Collections

Each season's collections are organized on a single screen. Pick a collection, browse the shows, press play. The app remembers where you left off.

Every Show Documented

Every show in every collection has a historical write-up — the venue, the city, what was happening with the band, what makes the recording worth hearing. Tap "About" on any show to read it.

Full Shows

Complete recordings from start to finish. Lock screen controls, background audio, automatic position saving. Close the app, come back tomorrow, pick up where you left off.

J-Cards

Every J-card from every collector's tape case has been photographed and is available in the app.

The Collectors

Each collection comes with background on the collector — who they are, where they're from, how they built their collection. Every collection has its own commissioned artwork.

iPhone only. No ads.
