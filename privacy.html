XLIIs — Privacy

Effective March 10, 2026

The Short Version

XLIIs doesn't collect personal data. No accounts, no ads, no sign-ups.

What's Stored on Your Device

Your listening history, favorites, and playback position are saved locally on your phone.

What's Sent to the Cloud

To let you resume where you left off, the app saves your playback position to a cloud database (Supabase). This is tied to a randomly generated ID — not your name, not your email, nothing personal.

The app also logs basic usage data — which collections and shows are played, and whether you're on iOS or the web. This helps us understand which collections people are listening to. Each entry is tied to the same random ID, not to you personally.

What the App Connects To

XLIIs streams audio from archive.org and syncs data through Supabase. No personal information is included in these requests.

What We Don't Do

No advertising. No third-party tracking. No data sold or shared. No data collection from children under 13.
